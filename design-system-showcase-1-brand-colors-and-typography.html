Line Heights

Tight (1.3)
Used for headings and short text blocks. This maintains a compact appearance while ensuring readability.
Normal (1.5)
Used for body text and general content. This provides comfortable reading with good spacing between lines.
Relaxed (1.7)
Used for large text blocks and improved readability. This spacing helps readers track lines in dense paragraphs.
